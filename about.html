About Golden Yarn Studio LLC

Crafting Quality Handbags with Passion and Tradition

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Golden Yarn Studio LLC began as a small family business with a big dream: to create beautifully crafted handbags that blend traditional craftsmanship with modern design.

Located at 1012 Marquez Place Ste. 106-B, Santa Fe, NM 87505, our studio has grown from a modest workshop into a trusted brand known for quality, style, and attention to detail. Each handbag we create reflects our passion for artistry, durability, and everyday elegance.

We are dedicated to using carefully selected materials and thoughtful design processes to ensure that every piece is not only stylish but also functional and long-lasting. Our commitment to craftsmanship and customer satisfaction has helped us build a loyal community of customers across the country.

Today, Golden Yarn Studio LLC continues to honor its roots while embracing innovation, ensuring that every handbag tells a story of creativity, dedication, and timeless design.
